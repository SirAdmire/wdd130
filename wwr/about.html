Exciting Expeditions

About Us

Our goal is to provide you with the best rafting experience possible. For over 30 years, our family-owned business has guided guests safely through unforgettable adventures. With a passionate and experienced team, we’re dedicated to helping you create lasting memories on the water.

History

Exciting Expeditions began floating rivers in 1995, quickly becoming known for safe, fun, and family-friendly rafting trips. Since then, we’ve grown into one of the region’s most trusted outdoor adventure providers, while still keeping the same welcoming, family spirit that started it all.

Adventure Awaits You!

Whether you’re a first-time rafter or a seasoned adventurer, Exciting Expeditions has a trip for you. Choose your level of excitement, bring your friends and family, and let us handle the rest!
