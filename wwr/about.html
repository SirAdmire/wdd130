White Water Rafting

Experience the thrill of white-water rafting like never before. At Exciting Expeditions, we combine adventure, safety, and breathtaking scenery to create unforgettable journeys on the river. Whether you’re seeking heart-pounding rapids or a calm family float, our expert guides are here to make every trip exciting, safe, and full of lasting memories

History

Exciting Expeditions began floating rivers in 1995, quickly becoming known for safe, fun, and family-friendly rafting trips. Since then, we’ve grown into one of the region’s most trusted outdoor adventure providers.

For over 30 years, our family-owned business has guided guests safely through unforgettable adventures, while keeping the same welcoming spirit that started it all.
