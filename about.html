Our History

Exciting Expeditions began floating rivers in 1995 and quickly became known for safe, fun, and family-friendly rafting trips. Since then, we’ve grown into one of the region’s most trusted outdoor adventure providers. For over 30 years, our family-owned business has guided guests safely through unforgettable adventures while maintaining the welcoming spirit that started it all.
